Creative Director

At SuccessField, we’re lucky that everyone within our walls thinks creatively, shares a passion for building communities, and understands the world of both big campaigns and social micro-content.

Who are you?

The creative direector is the creative lead on multiple lines of business. You will continually review and optimize the work your team produces, and work with leadership to establish effective ways to lead teams and process.

The task at hand

  • Help to lead and win new business pitches
  • Review and optimize the work your team produces, while making sure they are developing as creative problemsolvers.
  • Mentoring team leads (ACD's and creative leads)
  • Providing high level concept guidance and approvals
  • Working with Group Directors and Project Managers on internal team relationships and process

The ideal candidate looks like this:

  • Solid management experience leading large teams
  • Demonstrated success in leading multiple social media campaigns.
  • Gain client confidence as a leader who can manage work while successfully interpreting their brands
  • Be flexible with and open to new processes, platform changes, and emerging technologies
